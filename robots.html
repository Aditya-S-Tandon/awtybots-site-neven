Season 10 · Reefscape℠

Goldfire

Reefscape℠ · 2026
  • Swerve drivetrain running 4 Kraken X60 drive motors and 4 Falcon 500 steer motors, geared for ~4.8 m/s free speed with a compact low-center-of-gravity frame.
  • Dual-roller coral intake mounted under the bumper, collecting from the reef floor in a single pass; feeds directly into a vertical indexer with two compression rollers.
  • 2-stage elevator with a pivoting coral end-effector capable of scoring L1–L4. Algae removed from the reef via a spinning brush and scored in the processor or net.
  • Single-stage passive climb latches onto the cage bar in under 4 seconds; actuated by a servo-released spring latch.

Season 9 · Reefscape℠

Waverider

Reefscape℠ · 2025
  • West Coast drivetrain with 6 colson wheels, 4 Falcon 500 drive motors, geared for ~4.2 m/s — designed for pushing defense and stability on the reef.
  • Over-the-bumper ground intake with polycarb guide ramps funneling coral onto two compression rollers, feeding upward into a fixed-angle shooter.
  • Single-stage linear elevator reaching L3. Fixed coral shooter launches pieces at a tuned angle into L3 and L4 slots from a short standoff distance.
  • Chain-driven climb hooks deploy via a motor-driven crank; robot pulls itself up to low-bar hang in the final 15 seconds of endgame.

Season 8 · Crescendo℠

Jazz

Crescendo℠ · 2024
  • Swerve drivetrain with 4 Falcon 500 drive motors, ~4.5 m/s free speed, and a low-profile bellypan to keep CG below the note intake clearance height.
  • Under-bumper floor intake using 4 silicon-wrapped aluminum rollers powdered by 2 Falcon 500s; robot drives over notes at full speed without stopping.
  • 2 indexing rollers compress notes (≈270 mm width) into a slightly elliptical shape for tighter packaging, feeding into 4 vulcanized rubber rollers (2 top, 2 bottom) for the flywheel shooter.
  • Carbon fiber climbing arms latch onto the chain via hooks, then two winches retract to lift the robot; spring-loaded arms extend to enable trap scoring.
@ FIT Katy @ FIT San Antonio @ State Championships

Season 7 · Charged Up℠

Greenlight

Charged Up℠ · 2023
  • West Coast drivetrain, 6-wheel drop-center with 4 NEO motors and custom gearboxes; capable of sustained pushing force in the charging station zone.
  • Single-jointed arm with a pneumatic claw end-effector, picking up cones and cubes from the floor and human player station; arm reaches grid rows 2 and 3.
  • Auto-balance routine using a built-in gyro and PID controller to hold the charging station level within ±2° for maximum endgame points.
@ FIT Channelview @ FIT San Antonio @ State Championships

Season 6 · Rapid React℠

Topspin

Rapid React℠ · 2022
  • West Coast drivetrain, 6-wheel drop-center with 4 Falcon 500s; optimized for traversal speed between the fender and hub on the 2022 field.
  • Over-bumper cargo intake using compliant wheels and a pneumatically deployed arm; intakes from the floor or the human player station.
  • 2-stage flywheel shooter with hood angle adjustment via a servo; tuned to shoot cargo into the upper hub from the fender and launchpad positions.
  • Traversal climb accomplished with a telescoping arm and a second shorter hook to step from low to mid to high to traversal rungs.
@ FIT Channelview @ FIT Pasadena @ State Championships

Season 5 · Infinite Recharge℠

Ironhide

Infinite Recharge℠ · 2020 & 2021
  • West Coast drivetrain with 4 NEO motors and a shifting gearbox; two-speed transmission for sprint speed in open field and pushing power near the control panel.

Season 4 · Destination: Deep Space℠

Sideswipe

Destination: Deep Space℠ · 2019
  • West Coast drivetrain with pneumatic tires for traction on the hab platform; 4 CIM motors with a single-speed gearbox geared for ~3.8 m/s.
  • Pneumatic suction-cup mechanism picks up hatch panels from the loading station and scores them on the cargo ship and rocket ports at all three levels.

Season 3 · FIRST Power Up℠

Blurr

FIRST Power Up℠ · 2018
  • West Coast drivetrain, 6 CIM motors across 3 gearboxes; optimized for fast cube cycling between the portal and the switch/scale.
  • Side-roller cube intake with polycarb intake arms that compress around the cube; intakes from the floor in any orientation without alignment.
  • 2-stage telescoping elevator carrying the intake and shooter arm to scale height (~6 ft); cubes ejected via powered rollers at adjustable speed.
  • Single-rung climb: robot deploys a hook on the elevator and winches up in the final seconds to score ranking points from the scale bar.

# 8 days until DCMP

Today was a slower day as we mainly focused more on CAD and programming.

# CAD

- The rollers are pretty much done. We cut the plates and started disassembling our V1 roller robot. We also took apart our practice robot as that will be built into our dumper robot. Goldfire will not be touched except for some minor upgrades to the intake and swapping out motors (there’s a shooter motor we think is dying) with new ones so everything is fresh for DCMP.

- The dumper CAD is coming along as well. We have two versions, one without an adjustable hood and one with. We are short on hex shafts so it’s very possible we don’t even get this robot assembled. We’re hoping some come in stock soon or maybe we can get some from local teams. We are trying to finalize it to see how much we actually need before we start asking around.

# Programming

- Tomorrow programming will be going to Katy to test some auto aiming and auton routines on a full field. We are very grateful for this since we don’t have space to test these things at school and this has been our Achilles heel at our two events. We want this working from Qual match 1 at DCMP so we can be our best right away. Programming has been playing around with some shooting on the move but we don’t think that’s going to be super necessary with Goldfire. It might be with the dumper design with the adjustable hood but that’s low on the list of priorities.
